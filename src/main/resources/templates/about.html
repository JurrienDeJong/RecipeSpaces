Recipe Spaces is a company that optimizes products by providing insight into the effect of changes in the amounts of ingredients. One of our main goals to replace ingredients based on flavors and to visualize the effect of changes.
On our website can recipes and ingredients be searched to the find the best replacement for ingredients based on their flavor.
Besides finding replacements our website also visualizes an ingredient network based on the distances between ingredients. It also visualizes changes in the amount of ingredients by selecting two ingredients by interest.
On the website there are recipes with multiple variants. For these recipes there is a visualization showing the frequency of ingredients of the multiple recipes.
Our company provides the best insight into the effect of changes in ingredients.
